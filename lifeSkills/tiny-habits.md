## 2. How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P?
- M stands for motivation (the habit you want to start)
- A stands for ability (how easy the habit is to do). Easy behaviors have a high ability, and difficult behaviors have low difficulty)
- P stands for prompt (the cue that triggers the behavior)
- Behavior happens when the MAP happens together. You can apply this to shape your behavior.
