# Grit and growth mindset

## Grit
Angela's talk is about how grit (passion and perseverance) for long-term goals matters more than just talent or intelligence.

## Introduction to growth mindset
A growth mindset is the belief that abilities and intelligence are not fixed; rather, they can be developed through putting in effort and learning. Adopting a growth mindset allows us to learn from our failures and helps us improve continuously.

## Understanding Internal Locus of Control
### What is it?
The belief that one's own decisions, effort, and actions determine the outcomes and events in one's life.
### What is the key point of the video
By adopting this mindset, a person can take responsibility rather than blame an external factor. 
This will, in turn, keep the person motivated to move towards their goal.

## How to build a growth mindset
- Believe in your ability to figure things out
- Question your assumptions
- Take ownership of learning
- Honor the struggle
