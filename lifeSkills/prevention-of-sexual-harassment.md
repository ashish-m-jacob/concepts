# Prevention of sexual harassment

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
- Gender based insults
- Repeated requests for dates when the other person is not interested
- Sexual comments
- Using lewd language or making dirty jokes
- Gender stereotyping
- Eve Teasing

## 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
- Let the person performing the harassing act know that their behaviour is improper and that they need to stop doing it.
- Keep track of whenever this person harasses me
- If the harassment continues, report the incident to a member of the Internal Complaints Committee
- If the person is not relenting, file a complaint with the police.
