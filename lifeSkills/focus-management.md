# Focus Management
## Question 1: What is deep work?
Focusing completely on a task that is cognitively demanding with uninterrupted concentration
## Question 2: According to author, how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
- Quit social media
- Minimize low-value tasks
- Embrace boredom
- Carve out uninterrupted time blocks for deep work
## Question 3: How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
- Schedule time blocks for deep work
- Create rituals that allow me to enter deep work
- Do not use social media while I am at work
## Question 4: What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
- Reduces your ability to concentrate deeply
- It uses addictive designs to steal your attention
- Increases feelings of loneliness and isolation
- Creates anxiety
